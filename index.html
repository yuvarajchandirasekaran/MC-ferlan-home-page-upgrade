McFarlane Digital Launch Note

McFarlane Toys is extremely excited for the launch of our digital collectible platform. For years, some of our most dedicated fans have asked for a space that would allow them to expand their collections without fear of running out of room, so we created an experience to collect and showcase exact replicas of some of your favorite toys.

To celebrate, we are giving away up to three digital assets that will be dropped in your wallet on December 1st. All you have to do is spend $100 at the McFarlane Toys Store and use the code “DIGITAL”, and you will get one SPAWN LAUNCH SERIES digital figure, or if you spend $300, and use the code “SUPERFAN”, and you will receive Spawn, The Clown and The Redeemer. You will also receive a 5% rebate on your order.

McFarlane Toys wants to give our collectors an opportunity to experience the digital space for free, and get a glimpse of a new way to collect. We also have plenty of surprises in-store in the upcoming months, so please stay tuned and join us on this journey!
